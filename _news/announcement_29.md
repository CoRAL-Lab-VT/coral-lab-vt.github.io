Congratulations to Md. Shadman Sakib for his first Ph.D. publication in Advances in Water Resources! In this collaborative study, researchers from Virginia Tech and the University of Central Florida published the study: <a href="https://doi.org/10.1016/j.advwatres.2025.105108">"Breaking Down Annual and Tropical Cyclone-induced Nonlinear Interactions in Total Water Levels"</a> with a focus on the Chesapeake Bay in Virginia. Way to go Md. Shadman!
